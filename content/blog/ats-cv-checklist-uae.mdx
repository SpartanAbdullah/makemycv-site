## What is ATS and Why Does It Matter in UAE?

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) are software programs used by
companies to filter CVs before a recruiter reads them. In the UAE,
approximately 70% of large companies use some form of ATS.

Companies including Emirates, Etisalat (e&), ADNOC, Dubai
Government entities, and most multinationals operating in DIFC
use ATS to manage high application volumes.

## The 10-Point ATS Checklist

Use this before submitting any application:

**Format**
- [ ] Saved as PDF (unless employer requests Word)
- [ ] No tables, text boxes, or columns
- [ ] No images or graphics in the CV body
- [ ] Standard readable font (Arial, Calibri, 10-12pt)

**Content**
- [ ] Job title in your CV matches the role you're applying for
- [ ] Keywords from the job description appear naturally in your CV
- [ ] Standard section headings (Experience, Education, Skills)
- [ ] Dates in consistent format (Month Year – Month Year)

**Contact Details**
- [ ] Professional email address
- [ ] UAE mobile number with country code (+971)
